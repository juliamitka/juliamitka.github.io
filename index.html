About me

As a UX/UI Designer, I work on complex B2B products that require a balance between an aesthetic interface, a clear structure, and well-thought-out logic. In my design work, I focus on making even complicated processes simpler and easier for users to understand.

What I value most in design is bringing order to chaos: analyzing problems, structuring information, exploring different scenarios, and looking for solutions that are simple, useful, and well-grounded in the business context. I care about creating designs that are not only visually appealing, but above all clear and comfortable to use.

I hold a degree in Finance and Accounting from the Krakow University of Economics. I started my career in IT as a System Analyst at Comarch, and later continued developing it at Kaczmarski Group. It was there that I moved into UX/UI and helped shape a new design role within the organization.

Outside of work, I'm usually somewhere between planning my next trip, meeting friends, and enjoying a quiet evening at home. I like cooking, baking sourdough bread, and creating things in my own way - most recently, I've been developing a small project with personalized tote bags that I design and make myself.
